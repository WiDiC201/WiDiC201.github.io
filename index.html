About CodeCraft Camp

CodeCraft Camp is a student-led initiative designed to inspire the next generation of programmers. We believe coding should be accessible, fun, and empowering for everyone. Our hands-on approach combines real-world projects with fundamental computer science principles, training the kids that will changed the face of Technology.
